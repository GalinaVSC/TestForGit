Speed of light

The speed of light is not just a measure of how fast light travels, but it's also an incredibly critical component in many areas of physics, such as Einstein's theory of relativity. Light's speed serves as a cosmic speed limit-nothing with mass can travel faster than this universal constant. Understanding and studying the speed of light has allowed scientists to explore the very fabric of space and time, shedding light on the mysteries of our universe.
